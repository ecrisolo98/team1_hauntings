OVER WHICH YEARS AND MONTHS WERE BIGFOOT SIGHTINGS MOST COMMON?

WHAT ARE WE ABLE TO ANSWER WITH THIS VISUALIZATION?

To answer the question “Over Which Years and Months Were Haunted Sightings in States Most Common”, we made a Heatmap with years on the x axis and months on the y axis. The colors range from white to light pink to darker pinks to purples, representing more bigfoot sightings with each shade getting darker. Therefore, the heatmap shows us for example that the 2000s were a particularly busy year for bigfoot sightings with mostly purple rectangles in the heatmap. In contrast, the 1940s for example was a decade with far fewer sightings across the months.
